Indian Stock Market Options Trading

Step-by-Step Beginner to Advanced Guide

📌 What is Options Trading?

Options trading is a derivative strategy where traders buy or sell contracts based on underlying assets like stocks or indices.

Types: Call Option (Buy) & Put Option (Sell)

📊 Step 1: Understand Basics

Call Option: Expect market to go UP 📈
Put Option: Expect market to go DOWN 📉
Learn key terms:
  • Strike Price
  • Premium
  • Expiry Date
  • Lot Size

📈 Step 2: Choose Market Type

Index Options:
  • NIFTY 50
  • BANK NIFTY
  • FINNIFTY
Stock Options:
  • Reliance
  • TCS
  • Infosys

⚙️ Step 3: Open Trading Account

  • Choose broker (Zerodha, Upstox, Angel One)
  • Complete KYC
  • Activate F&O segment

💡 Step 4: Basic Strategies

Buy Call: Bullish Strategy
Buy Put: Bearish Strategy
Covered Call: Income Strategy
Straddle / Strangle: Volatility Strategy

📉 Step 5: Risk Management

  • Never risk more than 2% per trade
  • Always use Stop Loss
  • Avoid overtrading
  • Follow market trends

🧠 Step 6: Advanced Concepts

  • Option Greeks (Delta, Theta, Vega)
  • Implied Volatility (IV)
  • Open Interest Analysis

🚀 Pro Tips

  • Trade liquid options only
  • Focus on weekly expiry strategies
  • Avoid trading during news volatility
  • Practice in paper trading first
