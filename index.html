What is Options Trading?

Options trading allows investors to trade contracts that derive value from underlying assets like stocks.

Call & Put Options

Call options are used when expecting price rise, while put options are used when expecting price fall.

Key Terminology

Strike price, premium, expiry date, Greeks, IV, OI, and volume are essential concepts in options trading.
