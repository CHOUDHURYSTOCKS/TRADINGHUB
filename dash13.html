Intraday Option Trading Strategies (India)

🔹 Open Range Breakout (ORB)

How it works:

  • Mark first 15–30 minutes high & low
  • Trade breakout of that range

Setup:

  • If price breaks HIGH → Buy Call (CE)
  • If price breaks LOW → Buy Put (PE)

Tips:

  • Best time: 9:30–11:00 AM
  • Avoid false breakout → wait for candle close

🔹 VWAP Strategy

Setup:

  • Price above VWAP → Bullish → Buy CE
  • Price below VWAP → Bearish → Buy PE

Works best in: NIFTY Bank

🔹 Momentum Scalping

  • Look for strong trending candles
  • Enter on pullback or breakout
  • RSI: above 60 bullish, below 40 bearish
  • Target: 10–30 points

⚠️ Requires fast execution

🔹 Breakout with Volume

  • Identify consolidation zone
  • Wait for breakout with high volume
  • Breakout up → Buy CE
  • Breakdown → Buy PE

🔹 Support / Resistance Strategy

  • Bounce from support → Buy CE
  • Rejection from resistance → Buy PE
  • Use candlestick patterns

🔹 Intraday Short Straddle

  • Sell ATM CE + ATM PE
  • Best in sideways market

⚠️ High risk – only for experienced traders

🔹 1-Minute Scalping

  • Use 1-min chart
  • EMA (9 & 21) + VWAP
  • Goal: 5–10 quick trades

⚠️ High stress, not beginner-friendly

⚙️ Strike Selection
  • ATM → Best for beginners
  • ITM → Safer, less volatile
  • OTM → Risky, high reward
🕒 Best Trading Time
  • 9:30 – 11:30 AM → Strong trends
  • 1:30 – 3:00 PM → Breakout/reversal
  • Avoid first 5 minutes & expiry day (beginners)
🛑 Risk Management
  • Risk only 1–2% per trade
  • Always use stop-loss
  • Avoid overtrading
  • Don’t hold overnight
⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • Buying cheap OTM options
  • Ignoring time decay
  • Trading without confirmation
  • Revenge trading
💡 Pro Tips
  • Focus on one index (NIFTY 50)
  • Track global cues
  • Use proper position sizing
  • Maintain a trading journal
