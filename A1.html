Basics of Options

Options give you the right to buy or sell assets at a fixed price before expiry. Calls = buy, Puts = sell.

How Pricing Works

Premium depends on volatility, time decay (theta), strike price, and market demand.

Risk Management

Never risk more than 1–2% per trade. Always use stop-loss and hedge positions properly.

Advanced Strategies

Use spreads, straddles, and iron condors to profit from volatility and market direction.
