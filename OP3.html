📈 Options Trading Strategies

Beginner to Advanced Guide

🧭 What Are Options?

Options are contracts giving the right to buy or sell an asset.

  • Call Option: Right to BUY
  • Put Option: Right to SELL

🟢 Beginner Strategies

Long Call Bullish

  • Buy a Call option
  • Profit when price rises
  • Risk: Limited | Reward: Unlimited

Long Put Bearish

  • Buy a Put option
  • Profit when price falls

Covered Call Neutral

  • Hold stock + sell call
  • Earn premium income

Protective Put

  • Buy put for downside protection

🟡 Intermediate Strategies

Bull Call Spread

  • Buy lower strike call
  • Sell higher strike call

Bear Put Spread

  • Buy higher strike put
  • Sell lower strike put

Iron Condor Sideways

  • Sell OTM call & put
  • Buy further OTM options

Straddle

  • Buy call + put (same strike)

Strangle

  • Buy OTM call + put

🔴 Advanced Strategies

Butterfly Spread

  • Buy 1 ITM call
  • Sell 2 ATM calls
  • Buy 1 OTM call

Calendar Spread

  • Sell near-term option
  • Buy long-term option

Iron Butterfly

  • Sell ATM call & put
  • Buy OTM options

📊 Strategy Selector

  • Bullish → Long Call
  • Bearish → Long Put
  • Sideways → Iron Condor
  • Volatile → Straddle

⚠️ Risk Management

  • Risk only 2–5% per trade
  • Always use stop-loss
  • Avoid overtrading
