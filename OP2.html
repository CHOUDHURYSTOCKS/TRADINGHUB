Options Trading Strategies

From Beginner to Advanced — Step-by-Step Guide

📘 Beginner Level

```
Beginner

1. Long Call

Goal: Profit from rising prices.

Steps:

  • Choose a stock you believe will go up
  • Buy a Call Option
  • Wait for price to increase before expiry

Risk: Limited to premium paid

Reward: Unlimited

Beginner

2. Long Put

Goal: Profit from falling prices.

  • Select a weak stock
  • Buy a Put Option
  • Profit as price declines
```

📗 Intermediate Level

```
Intermediate

3. Covered Call

Goal: Generate income on owned stock.

  • Own the stock
  • Sell a Call Option
  • Collect premium regularly
Intermediate

4. Protective Put

Goal: Insurance against downside.

  • Hold stock
  • Buy a Put Option
  • Limit losses if price drops
Intermediate

5. Iron Condor

Goal: Profit from low volatility.

  • Sell OTM call & put
  • Buy further OTM call & put
  • Profit if price stays in range
```

📕 Advanced Level

```
Advanced

6. Straddle

Goal: Profit from big moves (any direction).

  • Buy Call and Put at same strike
  • Wait for strong movement
Advanced

7. Strangle

Goal: Cheaper version of straddle.

  • Buy OTM call and put
  • Profit from volatility spike
Advanced

8. Butterfly Spread

Goal: Profit from minimal movement.

  • Combine multiple options
  • Target narrow price range
```

💡 Pro Tips

  • Always manage risk (never risk more than 2–5% capital)
  • Understand Greeks (Delta, Theta, Vega)
  • Avoid trading during major news if inexperienced
  • Use stop-loss strategies
